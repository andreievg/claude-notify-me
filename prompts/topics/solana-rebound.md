## What to track
Signals that bear on the question: **"When is the broader crypto market — and Solana (SOL)
in particular — going to rebound?"** Track both market-wide drivers and Solana-specific
developments:

- **Price action & technicals** — notable SOL and BTC moves, support/resistance breaks,
  momentum or trend shifts, and any confirmed rebound or breakdown.
- **Macro & market structure** — Fed rate decisions, liquidity conditions, risk sentiment,
  BTC dominance, and "altcoin season" signals.
- **Solana ETFs & institutional flows** — spot SOL ETF filings, approvals, and launches;
  net inflows/outflows; and institutional accumulation or selling.
- **Solana fundamentals** — network activity, TVL, fees/real economic value, active
  addresses, developer activity, major ecosystem launches or partnerships, and any
  network outages or incidents.
- **Forecasts** — analyst and market views on the *timing* and *likelihood* of a rebound.

Exclude: price-only tickers with no context, unsourced hype, sponsored or "shill" content,
and giveaway/scam posts.

## Summarization criteria
- **Separate facts from forecasts.** Clearly label predictions as forecasts and attribute
  them to a named analyst/source.
- For any rebound prediction, state the **timeframe** and the **catalyst/reasoning** it
  depends on.
- Give brief **price context** (approximate SOL or BTC level, or % move) when an item is
  price-driven.
- Prefer primary/on-chain data and reputable outlets over social-media speculation.
- Stay neutral — **do not give investment advice** or make your own price calls.

## Severity rubric
Severity here reflects how significant an item is to the rebound question.
- **critical** — a major, market-moving catalyst: a spot SOL ETF approval/launch, a decisive
  macro shift (e.g. a major Fed pivot), large confirmed institutional adoption, or a sharp
  confirmed market-wide rebound or crash.
- **high** — a strong directional signal: significant ETF inflows/outflows, a major
  partnership or network milestone, a clear shift in analyst consensus, or a decisive
  technical breakout/breakdown for SOL.
- **medium** — moderate or mixed developments: an individual analyst forecast, a single-day
  move that stays within range, or incremental ecosystem news.
- **low** — routine commentary, a minor price wobble, or speculation without a concrete basis.
