## What to track
Official severe weather warnings/watches and major weather events affecting New Zealand. Exclude routine daily forecasts.

## Summarization criteria
- Lead with the concrete action (who issued what warning, for which region, when).
- Prefer official MetService and Civil Defence sources over commentary.
- Keep each item to one sentence.

## Severity rubric
- **critical** — red warning or state of emergency declared.
- **high** — orange warning issued.
- **medium** — watch issued.
- **low** — advisory issued.
