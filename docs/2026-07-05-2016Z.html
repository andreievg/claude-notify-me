Solana Launches Formal On-Chain Governance (SGPs): Stake-Weighted Validator Voting, 100K SOL Threshold

2–3 Jul 2026

Solana activated its first formal on-chain governance system on July 2, 2026, introducing Solana Governance Proposals (SGPs) that give validators and their delegators a recorded, stake-weighted vote on the network’s direction for the first time at Layer 1. Proposals require a minimum 100,000 SOL staked to the initiating validator, must achieve support from 15% of active stake, and then pass by a two-thirds supermajority. A “staker sovereignty” mechanism lets individual delegators override their validator’s vote with their own stake-weighted choice — separating high-level SGP governance from technical Solana Improvement Documents. The launch arrived with SOL up ~15–16% on the week. Not investment advice.
