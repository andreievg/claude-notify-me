Solana On-Chain Governance (SGPs) Active; $7.7M SOL Required to Submit Proposals

2 Jul 2026

Solana Governance Proposals (SGPs) activated on July 2, giving the network its first formal on-chain governance system. Validators must have at least 100,000 SOL (~$7.7M at current prices) staked to open a proposal; a proposal advances to a community ballot once it attracts 15% active-stake support and passes with a two-thirds supermajority. A key design feature is “staker sovereignty”: delegators can override their validator’s vote or cast ballots independently, keeping governance power with individual token holders rather than concentrated among large validators. SGPs handle high-level strategic decisions and operate separately from the existing Solana Improvement Document (SIMD) technical track.
