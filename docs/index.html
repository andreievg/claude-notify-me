CLARITY Act House Field Hearing Set for 17 Jul in New York; Senate Passage Odds at ~43%

13 Jul 2026

The House Financial Services Committee scheduled a field hearing titled “Building the Future of Finance: How CLARITY Act Unlocks Innovation” for 17 Jul in New York — the House’s first dedicated CLARITY Act event since the Senate version reached the floor calendar. The bill sorts digital assets into legal categories, giving CFTC jurisdiction over digital commodities (BTC, ETH) and leaving investment-contract assets with the SEC. It passed the House in July 2025 and cleared Senate Banking Committee 15–9 in May, but has stalled on the floor calendar over three disputes: an ethics standoff on officials’ crypto holdings, the Section 604 developer-protection provision splitting law enforcement, and a fight over stablecoin yield. The bill still needs ~7 Democratic votes for the 60-vote threshold before the August 8 recess; prediction markets price passage at ~43%. Not investment advice.
