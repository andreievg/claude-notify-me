Solana Launches On-Chain Governance (SGPs): 100K SOL Threshold, Two-Thirds Supermajority Required

2 Jul 2026

The Solana Foundation launched a fully on-chain governance system called Solana Governance Proposals (SGPs) on July 2, 2026, giving validators and SOL stakers a binding mechanism to vote on protocol-level decisions for the first time. Validators need at least 100,000 delegated SOL (~$7.7M) to initiate proposals; proposals advance to a stake-weighted vote once they clear 15% cluster support, and pass on a two-thirds supermajority. The framework introduces “staker sovereignty” — delegators can override or replace their validator’s vote — and lives at governance.solana.com. The system separates high-level strategic decisions (SGPs) from technical implementation (Solana Improvement Documents). Not investment advice.
