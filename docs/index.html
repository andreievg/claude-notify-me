Clearstream (Deutsche Börse) Adds SOL to MiCA-Compliant Regulated Custody — European Institutional Access

8 Jul 2026

Clearstream, the post-trade services arm of Deutsche Börse Group, expanded its regulated digital asset custody on July 8, 2026 to include Solana (SOL) alongside XRP, Stellar (XLM), Cardano (ADA), Litecoin (LTC), and Avalanche (AVAX). The service is specifically designed to meet institutional demand under the EU’s Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA) regulation, using Crypto Finance AG (another Deutsche Börse company) as sub-custodian. European institutions previously had to rely on offshore custodians or self-custody to hold SOL, introducing compliance, insurance, and audit complexities; Clearstream’s service resolves that gap within a familiar regulated environment on par with traditional securities custody. This is a significant infrastructure milestone for Solana’s institutional adoption in Europe. Not investment advice.
