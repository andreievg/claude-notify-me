Solana Launches On-Chain Governance (SGPs) July 2: 100K SOL Threshold, Staker Sovereignty Override

2 Jul 2026

The Solana Foundation activated the network’s first fully on-chain governance system on July 2, 2026, via Solana Governance Proposals (SGPs), live at governance.solana.com. Proposals require a minimum of 100,000 SOL staked to the initiating validator, must first achieve support from 15% of active stake, and then pass by a two-thirds supermajority with results recorded on-chain (stake-weighted, Merkle-verified). A key innovation — “staker sovereignty” — allows delegators to override their validator’s vote with their own stake-weighted choice, formally separating “should we do this” governance from technical Solana Improvement Documents. SOL was trading around $82 (+22% over the prior week) at the time of activation. Facts sourced from CoinDesk and Bankless.
